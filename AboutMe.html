Who am I?

Here's few words I would use to describe me.

Global Citizen

Whether it was participating in an exchange program in Japan, travelling across Europe, or working with people from half-way around the world, I love interacting with all kinds of cultures. The world is too big to limit yourself to one perspective.

Involved

I have always been one to be involved with multiple activities wherever I am. Somedays, I am a comedian performing with the GT improv-troupe, Let's Try This. Other days, I attend the video game developers club or the Ninendo company club. You could even find me at football games getting involved in the GT school spirit.

Inspired

Throughout my life, I have always been driven by my passion to understand and serve others. My high school fostered my passion to reach out into the world. Whether it was participating in speech and debate, or volunteering at my school, my curiosity in others drove me to be who I am today.
